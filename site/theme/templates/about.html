About

Fat Tony's is a podcast, community, and annual meet-up built to help people navigate the chaos of modern life, drawing inspiration from the ideas of Nassim Taleb and like-minded thinkers. This isn't about abstract theory - it's about practical application. How do you actually build an antifragile life in a complex world?

At its core, the Fat Tony's podcast features unfiltered conversations with real-world experts - not ivory-tower theorists. Topics include (but aren't limited to): Finance, convexity, non-linearity, deadlifts, fasting, psychology, cooking, poetry, risk, and the pursuit of freedom - or as we like to call it, f*ck you money.

Fat Tony's is more than a podcast - it's an annual meet-up and a decentralized tribe. The result? A high-signal group of people who trade ideas, collaborate, build, and push each other to live better lives. Members have written books, launched businesses, designed together, and forged real-world friendships.

We share ideas and support across this site, Twitter(X), our podcast, Discord server, and YouTube channel. In additional to our annual meet-up, We also host online events, talks, and book clubs. Follow us on Twitter(X) to stay in the loop.
